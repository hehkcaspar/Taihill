About Us

Taihill Venture is a Boston-based, industry-agnostic pre-seed deeptech fund, dedicated to pioneering breakthrough technologies that tackle longstanding challenges.

With a diverse and resourceful team, we are committed to empowering scientific founders to transform their innovations into tangible, real-world solutions.
