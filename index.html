About Us

We are a group of young emerging leaders from all over the world, who are eager to grow with entrepreneurs. We bring the future to the present by investing into breakthrough technologies and supporting extraordinary entrepreneurs.

In 2014, through a strong local network in the Boston area, our partners began doing angel investments in emerging technologies that represent progressive innovations. Skylight Investment was then established as our first VC fund in Boston in 2017, and we have since invested in over 70 companies.

After the success of Skylight, we launched our second fund in May 2020, brought in new partners from Europe, Midle East, India and China, and formally renamed it Taihill Venture.

As an early-stage and sector agnostic fund, we invest in a wide array of disruptive and impactful startups positioning us on the forefront of the evolving landscape of technology innovations. Having a diverse team of resourceful partners and advisors brings boundless vision and networks from across the globe.
